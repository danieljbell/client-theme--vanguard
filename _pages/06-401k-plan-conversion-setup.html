Conversion setup/Management

Helping you and your financial professional get started

A successful retirement plan onboarding experience starts with expert resources and a comprehensive process. Whether you have an existing plan or you’re setting up a new plan, you’ll get the support you and your financial professional need each step of the way.

  • Plan design guidance.
  • Dedicated installation coordinator.
  • Custom conversion timeline.
  • Quality control for data integrity.
  • Personalized enrollment booklets for your employees.

401(k) implementation milestones

  • Plan Establishment Kit (PEK) — 14 days from verbal agreement
  • Conversion and plan implementation 45–60 days1
  • Go live

You can expect your plan to go live 60–90 days after you have completed the PEK.2

Vanguard earned a 90% client approval rating on 401(k) plans converted in 2016.3

  1. A plan conversion can take an average of 45–60 days to complete; however, it may vary based on the time frame required by your prior recordkeeper.
  2. Dependent upon the receipt of timely and accurate plan data from your prior recordkeeper.
  3. Source: Ascensus New Business survey, 2016.
